# Undone

I watch over—
He was drunk.
A hiccup—\
in his holy silence.

I’ll sleep
Once he’s sober
When he sees
What he has done.

But till then
He must be—
Taken care of.
Cradled with 
Lullaby —
of crying children.

For when he’s drunk
He’s me
You
Himself.
